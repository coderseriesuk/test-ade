QUALITY EDUCATION

Make more schools available and accesible to the less priviledged ones. Stop overcrowding of pupils in schools.

Make more trained teachers available and equip them with standard teaching materials. Make school classrooms conducive for learning.

Tell Goverment and decision makers to help make education affordable around the world. Create an online group to help develop and promote information sharing around the world.

Donate educational supplies. Buy books and stationaries.
